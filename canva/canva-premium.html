Introducing Canva - Designing tool

Canva is a wonderful online tool that can help you design a wide range of things, from posters to business cards and trifold brochures to wedding invitations. Canva is a free service that requires a free account. You can sign up with an email account or by linking your g-mail or Facebook account to Canva. Canva is completely web based so the designs and projects you start on one computer can be accessed on from any other computer that can get onto the internet and to Canva.com.This handout will cover some of the basics to get you started.


What is Canva?

When we need graphics such as brochures, logos, social media posts and more, we usually hire a graphic designer. That or either we learn how to use Photoshop which will take months to master! That is until there was an app that changed all that!

Now, we have a drag and drop graphics builder such as Canva! This is the most popular graphics design app as it makes it incredibly easy for the user to create graphics. Thanks to the editable templates for different occasions, elements, and high-quality exports, you’ll never need to suffer with bad design ever again! Millions of people love using Canva for their business and personal needs because it’s so easy to learn. Even a beginner with 0 graphic design skills can make a decent design in minutes using Canva!
